A Historical Analysis of Black Players in Heart Football Club's 150-Year Journey

Data Visualization using Power BI

Project Summary

In the spirit of Black History Month, this project is a heartfelt tribute to the Black players who have been an integral part of Heart Football Club's remarkable 150-year journey. With a focus on celebrating their invaluable contributions, we embarked on a captivating exploration of the club's history, driven by the power of data and the visualization capabilities of Power BI.

Tools

Power BI
DAX
Flaticon

Insights

Black Player Achievements: The project shines a spotlight on the accomplishments of Black players, from iconic match performances to influential leadership roles within the club.
Cultural Impact: We delve into the cultural significance of Black players in the Heart Football Club, recognizing how they have enriched the club's identity.
Community Engagement: The data analysis also reflects on how Black players have engaged with and inspired the local community and the broader football world.
Equality and Inclusion:The project highlights the club's commitment to equality, inclusion, and diversity, symbolized by the impactful presence of Black players throughout its history.

Limitations

Data Availability: While the project strives to comprehensively celebrate Black players, historical data may have gaps, potentially affecting the full scope of the analysis.
Data Quality: As with any historical analysis, the accuracy and consistency of data can vary and may present challenges.

Data Source

The primary data source for this project remains the rich historical records and archives related to Heart Football Club, encompassing a century and a half of the club's heritage, including the contributions of Black players.
